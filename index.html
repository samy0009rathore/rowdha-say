Why Rowdha SAY?

We do not only build a concept for you, we custom build a team for your unique project.

With Rowdha SAY not every client gets the same team. Each proposal gets a ‘bespoke team’ that best fits the client’s needs.

Rowdha SAY is your one stop shop to SAY your story with unlimited talents.

Services

We help you SAY your story through

Creative Storytelling

With experience in crafting customized stories and performing them for prestigious brands such as Cartier, as well as inspirational keynote speeches for governments with audiences surpassing 2000 ppl, Rowdha enjoys making every performance a unique experience.

Creative Directing

Videos - Graphics - Photoshoots - Events
With experience working with private entities such as Majid Al Futtaim Group all the way to Ministerial projects, we create unique concepts that caters to your needs and deliver visual content and experiences bringing your vision to life with a bespoke team.

Creative Writing

With experience crafting scripts for powerful companies such as Mubadala and Emirates NBD (showcased in front of royalty) all the way to ideanty and product branding we write with the main goal of touching hearts and leaving a lasting impact.

Music Composing

With experience working with Government entities such as Abu Dhabi Culture and Tourism all the way to private project, we celebrate special occasions with heartfelt poetic lyrics, unique melodies and powerful songs.

Master of Ceremonies (MC)

With experience of MCing in the presence of royalty, Rowdha Alsayegh has the talent of engaging an audience and navigating unexpected challenges during events.

* All writing services include Arabic (MSA + Emirati) & English. If you require any more languages or dialect let us know and we find a way to SAY your story in your preferred language.
