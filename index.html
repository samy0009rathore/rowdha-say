Creative Storytelling

With experience in crafting customized stories and performing them for prestigious brands such as Cartier, as well as inspirational keynote speeches for governments with audiences surpassing 2000 ppl, Rowdha enjoys making every performance a unique experience.

Creative Directing

Videos - Graphics - Photoshoots - Events
With experience working with private entities such as Majid Al Futtaim Group all the way to Ministerial projects, we create unique concepts that caters to your needs and deliver visual content and experiences bringing your vision to life with a bespoke team.

Creative Writing

With experience crafting scripts for powerful companies such as Mubadala and Emirates NBD (showcased in front of royalty) all the way to identity and product branding, we write with the main goal of touching hearts and leave a lasting impact.

Music Composing

With experience working with Government entities such as Abu Dhabi Culture and Tourism all the way to private projects, we celebrate special occasions with heartfelt poetic lyrics, unique melodies and powerful songs.

Master of Ceremonies (MC)

With experience of MCing in the presence of royalty, Rowdha Alsayegh has the talent of engaging an audience and navigating unexpected challenges during events.
