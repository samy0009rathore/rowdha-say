Having a dream of giving a Ted Talk at 12 and preforming one at the age of 21, inspires Rowdha to believe that anything she transmits passion into is possible. Inspired by her Emirati culture while representing her country, government, and community in various events, gives her the right experience and motivation to stand in front of an audience and make a change.

Driven with passion in 2021 Rowdha lunched ‘Rowdha SAY’ a creative agency that aims to help brands SAY. Having the skills of an artist, writer, and a speaker, Rowdha SAY helps brands tell their stories through directing campaigns, art works, written scripts and more.
